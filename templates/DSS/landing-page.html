AI4RSE Lab: AI for Research Software Engineering

At the AI4RSE Lab at Wageningen University & Research, within the Information Technology Group, our research focuses on the automation of research software development. We explore how AI can enhance the efficiency, reliability, and reusability of research software. Guided by FAIR principles and Green AI, we design intelligent methods, build knowledge graphs, and develop decision-support systems to accelerate and sustain the research software lifecycle.
